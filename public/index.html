Lead Finder

Pulls active home-service businesses, filters for the ones worth calling, and ranks them by fit. Copy the number, drop it into Go High Level.

Hard filters: 4.5★+ · 10+ reviews · physical address · operational.
Lead score weights longevity, the 20–100 review sweet spot, recent activity, and weak web presence (your opening).
Market score blends city population (demand) with how open the competition is — high demand + beatable field = goldmine.
Maturity reflects review volume (a reliable "established" signal); exact business age isn't published by Google. Market demand is estimated from population.
